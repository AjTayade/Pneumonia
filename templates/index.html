Pneumonia Detection AI

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill with fluid, causing cough, fever, and difficulty breathing. This AI tool analyzes chest X-rays to identify visual indicators of this condition.

How to get a result:

  1. Upload an Image: Click or drag-and-drop an X-ray file onto the area below.
  2. Start Analysis: Click the "Analyze Image" button.
  3. View Prediction: The card will flip to show the result.
